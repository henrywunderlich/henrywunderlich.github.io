About Me

This program is similiar to the popular game "Flappy Bird". The goal of the game is fly the character through the green pipes in order to score points. If the character hits the pipes the game ends. Press the space bar to move the character up and release to make the character go down. Tapping the space bar is the best method to keep a constant height.
